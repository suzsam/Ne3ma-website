Who are we?

In line with the sustainable development goals, and specifically the first and the second goal, which is: 1-No Poverty and 2-Zero Hunger, we decided to create this initial website to find a solution to wasted food, whether from parties, conferences, weddings, or Ramadan tables. With the aim of donating this food to chaste families.

Our Goals?

Prevent or reduce the presence of excess food.By educating people about the
importance of saving on food and poor waste, and educating the community about bad habits and traditions that lead to food waste.
